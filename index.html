Frameworks

When you hear the word framework, you might think one or all of these three things:

  • Cool word!
  • A frame did what?!
  • I have no idea what this means.

Well, no worries! Let me explain what framework means.

Frameworks are platforms that you as a developer can use to build various applications. That way, you don't have to start from scratch when coding!

You could say that they are templates, or foundations, that developers use to build their project in or around. Super practical! If you are, like me, interested in Front-End development, relevant frameworks could be Angular or React for example.
